CORE IDEA (DO NOT BREAK)
================================================
P Profile = Digital Product Identity Platform

We turn receipts into verified product ownership.
Each product has:
- Proof (invoice)
- Warranty (manufacturer)
- Protection options (extended / insurance)
- Maintenance history
- Transferable ownership

PRODUCTS (CORE EXPERIENCE)
================================================
A) Products List
Each product card must show at a glance:
- Product name
- Status badge:
  - Under Manufacturer Warranty
  - Protection Active
  - Unprotected
- Expiry date if applicable

Tap → Product Profile

------------------------------------------------
B) Product Profile (MOST IMPORTANT SCREEN)
------------------------------------------------
Display information as sections (cards), not forms.

1) Product Identity
- Name
- Category
- Serial number (optional but recommended)

2) Manufacturer Warranty (Official)
- Provider (Apple, Samsung, etc.)
- Status: Active / Expired
- Expiry date

3) Protection Plan (Optional / Commercial)
- Status: Active / Available / Not eligible
- Provider (e.g. Extra)
- Coverage summary
- CTA (if available):
  “Protect this product”

IMPORTANT:
- NEVER call protection a “Warranty”
- Use “Protection Plan” or “Extended Protection”

4) Maintenance History
- Timeline view
- Each record includes:
  - Date
  - Type (Repair / Service / Part replacement)
  - Notes
- CTA:
  “Add maintenance”

Maintenance records are tied to the PRODUCT (via product ID + serial number if available).

5) Ownership & Transfer
- CTA:
  “Transfer / Sell product”
- Allow generating a secure transfer link.
- Buyer can accept and receive:
  - Product
  - Invoice (optional, redacted by default)
  - Maintenance history (optional)

6) Proof (Documents)
- Linked invoice(s)
- View-only
- Fullscreen viewer with zoom
- Invoice is always accessible after upload.

================================================
DOCUMENTS (SECONDARY)

- Invoice name (merchant)

================================================
ADD PRODUCT FLOW (CRITICAL UX)
================================================
Flow must be:
Simple → Review → Confirm

Step 1: Scan Invoice
- Camera or upload
- Clear instruction
- Continue

Step 2: Review Products
- Show invoice summary (read-only)
- Extract detected products into Product Cards
Each detected product:
- Toggle: Add to P Profile (default ON)
- Editable name
- Category
- Manufacturer warranty info (if known)
- Warranty provider inferred from merchant
- Optional: apply settings to all

NO global warranty form.

Step 3: Confirm & Save
- Clear summary:
  “You are saving:
   • 1 invoice
   • X products”
- CTA:
  “Save 1 invoice & X products”
- If X = 0:
  “Save invoice only”
